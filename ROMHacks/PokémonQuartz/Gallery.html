Pokémon Quartz — Pokédex Gallery

Pokémon Quartz introduced 386 new Fakemon, replacing every existing Pokémon as of Generation 3. Many Fakemon in Pokémon Quartz are direct stand-ins for official Pokémon.

This page shows the sprites for all of these Fakemon in National Pokédex order, separated by Generation.

You can click on each Pokémon to flip between its front and back sprite.
