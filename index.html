Welcome!

The Fakemon Library is a project that aims to document and classify as many Fakemon as possible while maintaining consistent quality and presentation.

"Fakemon" simply refers to Pokémon created by fans of the series, and they can be found in a variety of places: ROM hacks, fan games, art galleries, etc. Each project that contains Fakemon has its own Pokédex.

I do my best to correctly record all Pokédex data. That said, I'm not perfect and mistakes can happen. If you notice any errors and would like to submit a correction, or simply have any questions or comments, you can contact me here.

You can download PDF versions of the Pokédexes found here over at my Issuu page.

Lastly, if you wish, you may support me on Patreon.
